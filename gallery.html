About VOLTA

A space built on
conviction and
curiosity

Founded in Paris in 2009, VOLTA Gallery was born from a belief that art has the power to challenge, move, and transform. For fifteen years we have championed artists at the forefront of contemporary practice — providing not just exhibition space, but a long-term commitment to their development and visibility on the world stage.
